ABOUT UOMO

OUR STORY

In 1999, we opened our virtual doors as Europe's first online bookstore.

Now 25 years later, we are international and the store has 13.7 million active customers around the world, an assortment of 41 million items and 47,000 sales partners who sell their items through UOMO's platform (Survey date figures: December 31, 2024).

Our Mission

UOMO is committed to making a positive impact on society. In everything we do as a company, we strive for equality and equal opportunities for all. For example, we are committed to diversity and inclusiveness among employees by ensuring good working conditions for everyone working for us. In addition, we try to be as inclusive as possible towards our customers, for example by casting inclusive people for our advertisements and marketing. We also support charities, including emergencies and disasters. Furthermore, we develop and support learning programs aimed at increasing equal opportunities among children.

Our Vision

Reducing the production and use of stuff is a challenge for all of society. As one of the largest webshops of the world, we think it is important to take responsibility in this. We have an exemplary role and everything to make an impact with our daily actions. That is why we want to continuously improve our platform for a better future for all of us.

THE COMPANY

UOMO, the store of us all. That is the promise we work on together every day. And by together, we really mean together.

Of course, everyone from their own expertise and function but in teams where we reinforce or complement each other. That's why we look for talent with different experiences and backgrounds. That way we get a little better every day. And we grow together. As people, team and organization.
